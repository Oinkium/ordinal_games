Let $A=(M_A,\lambda_A,P_A)$ be an Abramsky-Jagadeesan game.  We give two different notions of a \emph{weakening} of $A$ (informally, a game that is `easier' than $A$ from the point of view of Player $P$):

\begin{enumerate}
